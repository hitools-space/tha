Tips for Summarizing Text Efficiently

Summarizing text can be tricky if you don’t follow a proper strategy. There are several stages involved in summarizing text. Here are the tips that you can follow to carry out this task efficiently: 

  • Read & Understand the Text

At first, you are required to go through the text you wish to summarize. This is the most important step for summarizing text efficiently, as without deeply analyzing text, your summary won’t be able to justify it. In addition, you shouldn’t confuse this process with mere reading, as you also need to understand the points discussed in the content.

  • Divide it into Sections

After reading and understanding the text, you need to split it into sections. It will make your text easily manageable and help you understand its main parts, which can give you an edge while summarizing it. This step is important because summarizing shouldn’t be based on a single section of your text. Instead, it needs to cover the information contained in your article or essay.

  • Highlight the Key Points of Each Section

Once you have divided the text into sections, you need to highlight the key points contained in them. Any factual or figurative data should be highlighted, as it will be essential for you to present such important details while summarizing the text.

  • Summarize Text Using Key Points

Now comes the actual text summarizing writing process in which you are required to use the key points that you highlighted in the previous step. It’s essential to cover all main points in the summary to present your text clearly and concisely. 

  • Cross-Check the Summarized Text

As you complete summarizing text, it’s essential to compare the summary with the actual text. When cross-checking the summary and original content, you need to look for the key points you missed and then summarize them as well.
